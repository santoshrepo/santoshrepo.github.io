Impact of COVID-19 on the way we work

The sudden onset of the global pandemic accelerates our progression towards ‘the future of work’. The Voop.Global aim is to track the change in our ways of working in Australia. This is not only our shift towards the new way of working but to track what comes afterwards. Most importantly, the opportunity exists from the online conversations taking place to make predictions about workplace culture as the lock-up moves through various stages and post-COVID. The COVID Dashboard tells the story of the changing workplace culture in Australia. Leadership teams, employees, associates and workplace researchers can use the Dashboard to acquire valuable insights about key factors impacting the workplace. Users get access to detailed information via the appropriate Visualizations and information hierarchy available from manipulating the filters. Key highlights of the Dashboard include workplace topics being discussed with respect to time.
